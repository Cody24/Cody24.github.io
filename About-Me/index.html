About Me

Welcome To My Webpage!
First, a little bit about me. My name is Cody Meyer and I am currently attending the University of Pittsburgh, and I will be graduating at the end of April with a BS in Economics. Ideally, I would love to find work in the city of Pittsburgh in the financial sector. To me, the financial sector is interesting on a number of levels. It is an industry that is constantly changing and adapting and it would provide a fascinating challenge to me. My years at the University have given me the requisite tools to deal with those challenges, and I am excited for the opportunity.
On a more personal note, there are other important aspects of life to me. I have a great group of friends that I love being around whenever I can. I also really enjoy going out and spending time trying to experience new things. We only get to live this life as an independent young person once, so I try and make the most of it.
