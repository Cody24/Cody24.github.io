About Me

Cody Meyer is a full-time student at the University of Pittsburgh, graduating in the spring of 2018. Upon graduating with a Bachelor of Science in the field of economics, he hopes to find employment in the Greater Pittsburgh area. He has experience using various statistical programs such as STATA and R, and brings a mentality of hard work and dedication to each task he undertakes. He also has a significant interest in the financial sector, believing the work to fascinating both for the sectors complexity, as well as the ever-changing challenge that it presents. On a more personal note, in his free time he enjoys spending time with his friends and attending athletic events. Cody has always enjoyed sports and they have played a significant role in his life to this point. To him, nothing is more enjoyable than going out and competing in some kind of athletic activity against friends.
